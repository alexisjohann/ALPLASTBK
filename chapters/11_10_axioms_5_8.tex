\paragraph{The Decay-Reactivation Cycle.}
Awareness naturally decays (AWX-A58) but can be reactivated (AWX-A59):

\begin{equation}
A(t) = A_0 \cdot e^{-\lambda t} + \sum_i \Delta A_i \cdot H(t - t_i)
\end{equation}

where $H$ is the Heaviside step function and $\Delta A_i$ are trigger-induced boosts.

\paragraph{Feedback Learning.}
Awareness learns from outcomes:

\begin{equation}
A_{SYS2}(t+1) = A_{SYS2}(t) + \rho_{feedback} \times \Delta Outcome_{impact}
\end{equation}

\paragraph{Mirror Coherence (AWX-A70a).}
A special axiom linking awareness to identity feedback:

\begin{equation}
A_X(t^*) = f(MirrorGap_t) \quad \text{where } MirrorGap = |I_t - S_t|
\end{equation}

\begin{itemize}
\item $\Delta I < 0.3$: Learning awareness (constructive feedback)
\item $0.3 \leq \Delta I < 0.6$: Transitional
\item $\Delta I \geq 0.6$: Defensive awareness (protection mode)
\item $\Delta I \geq 0.8$: Overload (Watchdog Alert)
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Cascade Dynamics.}
One person's awareness expression can trigger awareness in others:

\begin{equation}
\frac{dA_j}{dt} = \alpha \cdot A_i \cdot Influence_{i \to j} \cdot (1 - A_j)
\end{equation}

This explains viral awareness spread (e.g., social movements, moral panics).
