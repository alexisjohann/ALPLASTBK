### 10. Aggregierte Wohlfahrtsfunktion
$$Q(C^*) = \sum_{i \in \{INU,KNU,IDN\}} \sum_{d \in FEPSDE} \sum_{t=0}^{3} \omega_i \cdot \delta_d(\Psi)^t [G_{i,d,t} - \lambda_d(\Psi) \cdot P_{i,d,t}]$$

Parameter:
- ω_i: Category weights
- δ_d(Ψ): Dimension-specific discount factors
- λ_d(Ψ): Dimension-specific loss aversion
