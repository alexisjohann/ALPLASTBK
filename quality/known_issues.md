## Methodological Limitations

### 1. Empirical Validation Scope
**Status:** Acknowledged in paper (§14, Appendix V)

The 8Ψ empirical validation has inherent limitations:
- Sample sizes range from N=18 to N=142
- Cross-sectional analysis (no panel data)
- Ψ dimensions may be endogenous to outcomes
- Additive model may miss interaction effects

**Mitigation:** Limitations explicitly stated in Appendix V §5

---

### 2. FEPSDE Dimensionality
**Status:** Theoretical claim, limited empirical testing

The 144-component FEPSDE matrix is theoretically derived but:
- Not all components empirically validated
- Some dimensions may be redundant
- Weighting across dimensions unspecified

**Mitigation:** Framework presented as theoretical scaffold, not empirical fact

---

### 3. C* Identification
**Status:** Mathematical proof complete, empirical identification challenging

The reference structure C* is:
- Theoretically well-defined (three derivations converge)
- Difficult to estimate from observational data
- May require experimental elicitation

**Mitigation:** Paper focuses on theoretical properties; empirical identification left for future work
