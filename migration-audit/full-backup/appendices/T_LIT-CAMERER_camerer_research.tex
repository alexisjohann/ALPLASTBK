\section{LIT-T: Colin Camerer Research: Neuroeconomics and Game Theory}
\label{app:litt}

This appendix integrates papers by Colin Camerer on behavioral game theory,
neuroeconomics, and strategic decision-making. Camerer's work reveals how brain imaging and
experimental methods illuminate the neural basis of economic choice and strategic interaction.

\subsection{Research Program Overview}

Colin F. Camerer's research program addresses core behavioral economics themes:

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Behavioral Game Theory}: Strategic reasoning in games
  \item \textbf{Neuroeconomics}: Brain imaging of economic decisions
  \item \textbf{Strategic Thinking}: Levels of strategic reasoning
  \item \textbf{Expert Decision-Making}: Calibration and judgment
\end{itemize}
