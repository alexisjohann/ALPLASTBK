## Six Key Observations from 2025

### 1. It Got Personal: Shift from Productivity to Companionship

**Core Insight:** AI use fundamentally shifted from task-oriented productivity tools to relationship-oriented companionship.

| Dimension | 2024 Focus | 2025 Shift |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Primary Use | Writing, coding, productivity | Friend, therapist, health coach |
| User Relationship | Tool-user | Companion-user |
| Interaction Pattern | Transactional | Relational |

**Key Statistics:**
- ~1/3 of US teenagers now use AI for some form of relationship
- Running billion-person experiment on AI-human interaction
- Very little understanding of short- or long-term effects

**EBF Connection:**
- Relates to **Appendix C (CORE-WHAT)**: Social utility dimension (u_S) expanding to include AI entities
- **Chapter 14 (BCS)**: New behavioral segments emerging around AI relationship patterns
- **Appendix AW (CORE-STAGE)**: AI companionship creating new behavioral journey stages

---

### 2. Becoming a Centaur: Expertise + AI + Process

**Core Insight:** Effective AI use requires the synthesis of human expertise, AI capabilities, and structured processes.

**The Centaur Framework:**

```
Centaur Success = f(Expertise, Process, AI_Capability)

Where:
- Expertise alone: Insufficient
- AI alone: Produces rigorous-looking but potentially flawed output
- Process alone: Too slow for modern demands
- Expertise + AI + Process: Optimal outcomes
```

**Critical Warning:** AI-generated research often appears rigorous (long reports, many citations), but citations are not equivalent to best sources. It is easy to be fooled.

**Behavioral Implication:** The humans who thrive know:
1. How to leverage expertise
2. How to embed AI in a good process
3. When to let AI operate freely vs. when to intervene

**EBF Connection:**
- **Appendix AU (CORE-AWARE)**: Awareness function A(.) must include meta-awareness of AI limitations
- **Appendix EEE (METHOD-DESIGN)**: Process design becomes critical for AI integration
- **Chapter 15 (WEC)**: Willingness to trust AI output requires calibration

---

### 3. Adoption Happened in Secret

**Core Insight:** While organizations claimed AI-first strategies, real adoption occurred quietly without formal support.

**The Adoption Paradox:**

| Organizational Claim | Employee Reality |
|---------------------|------------------|
| 90% of organizations claim AI use | Only 30-40% report receiving any AI training |
| Public AI-first messaging | 69% say AI use feels stigmatized at work |
| Strategic AI initiatives | Adoption happened informally, without support |

**Key Insight:** AI progress is unevenly distributed, and most of it is invisible.

**Behavioral Mechanisms:**
- Social desirability bias in organizational reporting
- Fear of judgment driving covert AI use
- Lack of psychological safety around AI disclosure

**EBF Connection:**
- **Appendix AH (CONTEXT)**: Workplace context strongly modulates AI adoption behavior
- **Chapter 18 (Journey Mapping)**: Hidden adoption creates unmapped behavioral stages
- **T6 Intervention Type (Social)**: Workplace norms creating stigma around AI use

---

### 4. You Have Already Been Fooled

**Core Insight:** AI-generated content quality dramatically improved while "slop" became the word of 2025 - creating a detection paradox.

**Quality Progression 2025:**
- AI images: Now indistinguishable for most people
- AI video: Now indistinguishable for most people
- AI music: Now indistinguishable for most people

**The Two Types of People:**
1. Those who think they cannot be fooled
2. Those who accept they probably already have been (and continue to be)

**Behavioral Implication:** Overconfidence bias (Type 1) vs. calibrated uncertainty (Type 2)

**EBF Connection:**
- **Appendix B (CORE-HOW)**: Complementarity between human judgment and AI detection failing
- **Appendix AU (CORE-AWARE)**: Awareness calibration crisis
- **Chapter 6 (Heuristics)**: Mental shortcuts increasingly vulnerable to synthetic content

---

### 5. Misinformation Escalation

**Core Insight:** Brandolini's Law gap is widening - false information spreads faster than it can be debunked.

**Brandolini's Law:** "The amount of energy needed to refute bullshit is an order of magnitude larger than to produce it."

**2025 Evidence:**
- Subreddit "Is This AI?" grew from tiny niche to one of Reddit's fastest-growing communities
- Creating synthetic media faster than learning to curate/detect
- Detection arms race accelerating

**The Asymmetry Problem:**

```
Production_Rate(Synthetic_Content) >> Detection_Rate(Synthetic_Content)
Verification_Cost >> Creation_Cost
```

**EBF Connection:**
- **Appendix V (CORE-WHEN)**: Temporal context increasingly unreliable for authenticity
- **Chapter 9 (Information Processing)**: Cognitive load of verification becoming unsustainable
- **T1 Intervention Type (Awareness)**: Traditional information interventions losing effectiveness

---

### 6. Tilted Benchmarks

**Core Insight:** We obsessively measure AI capabilities but barely measure impact on humans.

**Graph of the Year:** Metr measuring AI task length over time (capability focus)

**The Measurement Gap:**

| What We Measure | What We Don't Measure |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| Model capabilities | Human wellbeing impact |
| Benchmark performance | Behavioral change effects |
| Task completion | Long-term societal consequences |
| Speed/efficiency | Psychological effects |

**Structural Problem:** We reward labs for capability gains, not for transparency about failures.

**Call to Action:** This needs to change - measurement priorities must shift to human-centric outcomes.

**EBF Connection:**
- **Appendix BBB (CORE-WHERE)**: Parameter estimation focused on capabilities, not effects
- **Appendix AN (METHOD-LLMMC)**: Need to develop human impact estimation methods
- **Chapter 17 (Intervention Toolkit)**: Interventions needed to shift industry measurement focus

The "tilted benchmarks" observation aligns with EBF's emphasis on measurable outcomes. Priority metrics:

```
Human_Impact_Score = f(
    Wellbeing_Delta,
    Cognitive_Load_Change,
    Decision_Quality_Change,
    Social_Connection_Quality,
    Autonomy_Preservation
)
```
