## PART 2: THEORY-EVIDENCE MAPPING

### Theory Category MS-CM (Complementarity) - 7 papers

**Fehr & Schmidt Complementarity Theory** applied to Finance:

```
Social Preferences (U_S) × Financial Incentives (u_F)
    ↓ γ_SF (Crowding-out Risk)
Result: Trust Recovery Effectiveness
```

| Paper | Theory | γ Parameter | Implication |
|-------|--------|-----------|------------|
| fehr2009time | Fairness + Incentives | γ ≈ **-0.15** | Incentives can undermine fairness |
| fehr2007contract | Trust + Motivation | γ ≈ **+0.35** | Fair contracts reinforce trust |
| fehr2004gifts | Social + Financial | γ ≈ **+0.25** | Gift exchange boosts reciprocity |
| falk2021reciprocal | Reciprocity + Institutions | γ ≈ **+0.40** | Institutional design matters |

**Implication for M3 (Trust Recovery):**
- Direct financial incentives can **backfire** (γ < 0)
- Procedurally fair contracts **strengthen** trust (γ > 0)
- Combination of fairness signals + modest incentives **optimal** (γ ≈ +0.25)
