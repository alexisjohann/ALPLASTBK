Key Insights
🔑 ΨF is the Universal Enabler
Factor Flexibility interacts positively with nearly all other dimensions. Without the capacity to act, other contextual advantages cannot be leveraged.
🔄 Institutions ↔ Social Capital
Formal rules and informal trust are substitutes (β = −0.055). Countries optimize across this tradeoff based on historical path dependencies.
📉 Information Has Diminishing Returns
ΨK × ΨE = −0.075. Information interventions work best where baseline information is low—not in already-informed contexts.
