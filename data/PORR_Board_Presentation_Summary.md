## Executive Overview

PORR AG stands at an inflection point. As Europe's leading focused construction company with €6.2B revenue and 21,000 employees, we have the opportunity to transform from a regional consolidator into a **digital, sustainable, European market leader** by 2035.

### Strategic Vision: "Digital Infrastructure Leader"

**Target State 2035:**
- **Revenue:** €9.5B (+53% vs 2024, 4.1% CAGR)
- **EBIT Margin:** 4.0% (+140 bps improvement, from 2.6%)
- **Market Position:** Top 5-7 European contractor; Tier-1 in home markets
- **Employees:** 26,000 (+5,000 from 2024)

### 2. Strategic Four Pillars (Balanced Portfolio)

Our base case rests on **4 interconnected strategies** (not single bets):

#### Pillar 1: Digital Transformation (35% weight)
- **Investment:** €200M capex over 11 years
- **Targets by 2035:**
  - BIM adoption: 95% of projects (vs 35% today)
  - IoT deployment: 200+ projects (vs 2 today)
  - Cost reduction: -35% project costs via AI scheduling
  - Schedule accuracy: ±5% (vs ±12% today)
- **Margin Impact:** +0.6pp (largest driver)

#### Pillar 2: Sustainability Roadmap (30% weight)
- **Investment:** €180M capex
- **Targets by 2035:**
  - CO₂ neutral: 80%+ of projects
  - Renewable energy: 75% of fleet
  - Green premium pricing: 5-8% uplift
- **Margin Impact:** +0.4pp (embedded in premium)

#### Pillar 3: Regional Consolidation (25% weight)
- **Investment:** €200M capex (M&A)
- **Targets by 2035:**
  - CEE market share: 15% → 22%
  - 3-5 regional acquisitions integrated
  - 2,500 → 3,000 CEE employees
- **Margin Impact:** +0.2pp (fixed cost absorption)

#### Pillar 4: Specialist Infrastructure (10% weight)
- **Investment:** €80M capex
- **Targets by 2035:**
  - Alpine tunneling: €250M+ annual revenue
  - European corridors: Brenner BBT, Lyon-Turin
  - Premium margins: 5% EBIT (vs 2.6% blended)
- **Margin Impact:** +0.2pp

**Total Margin Bridge: 2.6% → 4.0% (+1.4pp)**

### 3. Margin Expansion: The Math

```
2024 EBIT Margin:           2.6%
├── Digital productivity:   +0.6pp (BIM, IoT, AI)
├── Segment mix:            +0.4pp (civil > residential)
├── Operational leverage:   +0.2pp (revenue growth >cost)
├── Prefabrication:         +0.1pp (labor efficiency)
└── Supply chain:           +0.1pp (optimization)
────────────────────────────────
2035 EBIT Margin:           4.0%
```

**Absolute EBIT Growth:** €158M (2024) → €380M (2035) = +€222M (+140%)

## Board Decision & Recommended Action

### Recommendation: **STRONGLY APPROVE BASE CASE STRATEGY**

**Rationale:**
1. **Balanced growth** across 4 pillars (not single-bet risk)
2. **Achievable execution** (€82M/year capex; proven capabilities)
3. **Margin expansion justified** (2.6% → 4.0% via structural improvements)
4. **Competitive positioning** (top 5-7 European contractor)
5. **Stakeholder alignment** (digital-first, ESG-first, capital discipline)

### Approval Conditions:
- ✅ Quarterly Board tracking vs milestones
- ✅ Go/No-Go review at decision points (Q3 2026, Q4 2026, Q2 2027)
- ✅ Monthly capex tracking vs €82M annual budget
- ✅ Risk Management Committee oversight

### Alternative Consideration:
- **Monitor** infrastructure spending pipeline for upside case triggers
- **Preserve capex optionality** in Phase 2 (€360M flexible for acceleration)
- **Review annually** in context of EU Next Generation fund execution
