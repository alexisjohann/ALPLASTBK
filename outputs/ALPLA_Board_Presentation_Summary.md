## SLIDE 7: KEY FINDINGS & RISKS

### ✅ Positive Catalysts
1. EU recycling mandate = forced capex investment
2. Circular economy = customer differentiation
3. Asia-Pacific expansion = new customer bases
4. Vertical integration = cost advantage
5. Pharmaceutical division = premium pricing
6. Paboco innovation = new product category

### ⚠️ Risk Factors
1. Commodity plastic price volatility
2. Competitive response (Amcor, Berry)
3. Technology delays (Paboco timeline pushes)
4. Emerging market execution (political risk)
5. Capex requirements (€50M/year discipline needed)
6. Customer concentration unknown (Coca-Cola dependency?)

### 🛡️ Mitigation Strategies
- Focus on organic growth (not M&A) for execution quality
- Double recycling capacity to achieve 700K tonnes by 2030
- Diversify geographically to reduce customer risk
- Invest in R&D for innovation differentiation
