## TEIL 5: TECHNOLOGY DEEP DIVES

### 5.1 ERP Strategy: SAP S/4HANA

**Why SAP?**
- Industry leader for manufacturing (90% of F500 use SAP)
- Strong in recycling/sustainability tracking
- Global scalability (200+ plants)
- Advanced analytics built-in
- Competitor to Oracle Cloud ERP

**Rollout Approach:**
1. **Pilot Phase (2025-2026):** 3-5 plants (Thailand, Germany, Mexico)
2. **Wave 1 (2027-2028):** Europe (50 plants)
3. **Wave 2 (2028-2029):** Americas (50 plants)
4. **Wave 3 (2029-2030):** APAC/AMET (50 plants)
5. **Stabilization (2030-2035):** Optimization, continuous improvement

**Cost: €35M over 3 years** (licenses + implementation + training)

### 5.2 IoT Strategy: Production Monitoring

**Sensors by Plant Type:**
- Blow-molding: Temperature, pressure, cycle time, defect rate
- Injection-molding: Injection pressure, cooling time, mold temperature
- Recycling: Feed rate, temperature, contamination level
- Pharma: Cleanroom conditions, environmental monitoring

**Data Integration:**
- Local IoT gateway → Cloud data warehouse
- Real-time dashboards (production, quality, downtime)
- Alerts for anomalies (temperature out of range, defect spike)

**Target Coverage:**
- 2025: 10 plants (pilot)
- 2027: 50 plants
- 2029: 150 plants
- 2035: 200 plants

**Cost: €25M over 11 years** (sensors, gateways, software licenses)

### 5.3 Cybersecurity Strategy: Zero-Trust

**Principles:**
- Never trust, always verify
- Micro-segmentation (250 plants treated as separate security zones)
- Multi-factor authentication for all access
- Continuous monitoring and anomaly detection

**Implementation Phases:**
1. **Assessment (2025):** Audit all 250 plants
2. **Hardening (2025-2026):** Firewalls, VPNs, encryption
3. **Detection (2027-2028):** SIEM (Security Information & Event Management)
4. **Response (2029-2035):** Automated incident response

**Cost: €20M over 11 years** (tools, personnel, consulting)

### 5.4 Data & Analytics Strategy: AI-Driven

**Tier 1 (2025-2026): Descriptive Analytics**
- Dashboards (production, sales, financial KPIs)
- Reports (daily, weekly, monthly)

**Tier 2 (2027-2028): Predictive Analytics**
- Demand forecasting (machine learning)
- Quality prediction (defect rates before they happen)
- Churn prediction (customer retention)

**Tier 3 (2029-2035): Prescriptive Analytics**
- Pricing optimization (AI recommends prices)
- Supply chain optimization (AI recommends shipment routing)
- Maintenance prediction (AI schedules maintenance)

**Platform: Tableau/PowerBI for visualization + Python/R for ML models**

**Cost: €15M over 11 years** (tools, data scientists, infrastructure)
