# ALPLA ORGANIZATIONAL DESIGN MODEL
## From €4.9B (2024) to €9.9B (2035): Three-Phase Evolution

**Document:** Comprehensive organizational architecture for strategic growth
**Framework:** Evidence-Based Organizational Behavior (EBOB)
**Period:** 2024-2035 with Phase-wise Milestones
**Version:** 1.1 | January 19, 2026

> **Update 2026-01-19:** Incorporates 2025 actual data (€5.2B revenue, 25,500 employees, 206 plants) and confirmed regional leadership appointments (Roland Wallner WEEU, Ronald Tichelaar APAC).

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

To achieve €9.9B revenue by 2035 (+101%), ALPLA must evolve from a **globally decentralized family business** to a **strategically coordinated innovation-driven organization**. This transformation requires:

- **Headcount growth:** 24,350 (2024) → 25,500 (2025 actual) → ~38,500 employees (2035)
- **Regional rebalancing:** Europe-centric → Asia-Pac/Emerging markets-heavy
- **Functional expansion:** R&D/Innovation as strategic center; Recycling as peer division; Digital/IT elevated
- **Governance evolution:** Board remains lean (5 members) but strategic committees expand
- **Talent model shift:** Technical specialists (recycling, innovation) become key differentiator
