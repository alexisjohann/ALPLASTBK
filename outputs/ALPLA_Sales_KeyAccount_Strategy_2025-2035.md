## TEIL 3: PHASE 1 (2025-2026) - MARKET FOUNDATION

### 3.1 Strategic Objectives

**Goal:** Establish market entry foundation for APAC/AMET; launch recycled materials channel

### 3.2 Segment Strategy - Phase 1

#### A. BEVERAGE SEGMENT (Current Leader)

**2024 Revenue:** €1.5B | **2026 Target:** €1.6B (+6.7% growth)

**Strategy:**
- Maintain top 5 global customers (Coca-Cola, PepsiCo, Danone)
- Expand secondary regional bottlers
- Upsell recycled content (rPET 25% by 2025)

**Key Actions:**
1. Coca-Cola global account: Negotiate contracts with 25% recycled content premium
2. Regional bottlers: Expand in LATAM, APAC (Vietnam, Indonesia, India)
3. Upsell PET preforms: Higher-margin product mix
4. Launch Paboco: Early customer trials (beverage brands testing paper bottles)

**Sales Approach:**
- Existing 20-30 key account managers
- Regional account executives (+20 new hires for APAC/AMET)
- Technical support: Bottle design, sustainability claims

#### B. PHARMA SEGMENT (Highest Margin)

**2024 Revenue:** €0.7B | **2026 Target:** €0.75B (+7.1% growth)

**Strategy:**
- Geographic expansion: India, Egypt, Southeast Asia
- Premium positioning: Sterile, specialized packaging
- Regulatory partnerships: FDA, EMA approval support

**Key Actions:**
1. India pharma market: Establish in-house plants (Hyderabad tech center support)
2. Egypt pharma: Expand post-acquisition
3. Southeast Asia: New pharma customer base (Vietnam, Thailand)
4. Specialty markets: Biosimilars, cell/gene therapy (high margin)

**Sales Approach:**
- Dedicated pharma sales team (+10 new hires)
- Regulatory affairs specialist (new hire)
- Direct relationships with pharma procurement

#### C. RECYCLED MATERIALS - NEW CHANNEL

**2024 Revenue:** €0.4B | **2026 Target:** €0.45B (+12.5% growth)

**Strategy:**
- Launch rPET/rHDPE as standalone channel (not just in-house)
- Target: Automotive OEMs, appliance manufacturers, CPG convertors
- Positioning: Sustainability = cost advantage + premium pricing

**Key Actions:**
1. New sales team: 3-5 dedicated recycled materials account executives
2. Marketing: "Circular packaging" as brand positioning
3. Contracts: Multi-year rPET supply agreements with major converters
4. Capacity: Ensure 350K tonnes available (vs. current demand)

**Target Customers:**
- Automotive OEMs (Ford, VW, Renault): Interior trim plastic
- Appliance (Bosch, LG, Whirlpool): Housing plastic
- Fashion (apparel polyester fiber): Emerging market
- Industrial converters (resin buyers)

**Pricing:** 5-10% premium vs. virgin plastic (sustainability value)

#### D. INDUSTRIAL SEGMENT

**2024 Revenue:** €0.8B | **2026 Target:** €0.85B (+6.25% growth)

**Strategy:**
- Maintain existing customer base
- Expand capacity for new applications (large containers, industrial packaging)

#### E. OTHER SEGMENTS

**2024 Revenue:** €0.3B | **2026 Target:** €0.32B

**Strategy:** Steady-state maintenance

5. **Supply Constraints:** Limited recycling capacity, premium demand

**Estimated Price Growth:** 2-3% annually over inflation (2025-2035)
