# ALPLA HR-STRATEGIE
## 2025-2035: Talent Acquisition & Organizational Development für +58% Growth

**Dokument:** Comprehensive HR Roadmap für 14,150 neue Mitarbeitende
**Framework:** Talent-Driven Growth, Succession Planning, Organizational Health
**Period:** 2025-2035 mit Phase-weise Rekrutierung
**Version:** 1.0 | Januar 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Um von 24,350 (2024) auf 38,500 (2035) Mitarbeitende zu wachsen, braucht ALPLA eine strategische HR-Transformation:

- **Hiring Target:** +14,150 Mitarbeitende (+58%) = 1,286/Jahr durchschnittlich
- **HR Organization:** 600 (2024) → 1,200 (2035) Mitarbeitende (+100%)
- **Payroll Investment:** €1.58B (2024) → €2.70B (2035) = +€1.12B/Jahr
- **Retention Focus:** <10% turnover on critical roles (vs. industry 15%)
- **Succession Planning:** Non-family CEO ready by 2035
- **Regional Focus:** APAC +140%, AMET +380% (vs. Europe +32%)

## TEIL 7: COMPENSATION & BENEFITS

### 7.1 Global Compensation Strategy

**Principles:**
1. Competitive with local market (75th percentile)
2. Equitable across regions (equivalent levels)
3. Performance-based (25% at risk)
4. Transparent (all employees know pay bands)

### 7.2 Bonus Program (Performance-Linked)

| Level | Base | Bonus (% of base) | Bonus Drivers |
|-------|------|-------------------|---|
| **Plant Workers** | €25-35K | 10% | Safety, quality, productivity |
| **Supervisors** | €50-70K | 15% | Team KPIs, engagement |
| **Managers** | €80-120K | 20% | Department P&L, retention |
| **Directors** | €120-180K | 25% | Division revenue, EBITDA |
| **C-Suite** | €150-250K | 30% | Company revenue, EBITDA, organic growth |

### 7.3 Benefits Evolution

**Phase 1 (2025-2026):**
- Healthcare (all regions)
- Retirement (all regions)
- Parental leave (all regions)
- Flexible work (where applicable)

**Phase 2 (2027-2029):**
- Professional development budget (€1K/employee/year)
- Tuition reimbursement (advanced degrees)
- Mental health support (global EAP)
- Stock options (if company considers exit)

**Phase 3 (2030-2035):**
- Wellness programs (health tracking)
- Sabbatical program (after 10 years)
- Childcare assistance (urban locations)
- Retirement planning (executive level)
