Title: The Role of Reciprocity in Economics
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Nature Human Behaviour
Year: 2015

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This Nature Human Behaviour article examines the role of reciprocity in
economics. We review evidence for reciprocal behavior and discuss its
implications for economic theory and institutions.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. RECIPROCITY EVIDENCE
   - Positive reciprocity robust
   - Negative reciprocity prevalent
   - Context modulates strength

2. ECONOMIC IMPLICATIONS
   - Contract enforcement
   - Labor markets
   - Public goods provision

3. INSTITUTIONAL DESIGN
   - Harness reciprocity
   - Avoid crowding out
   - Build trust
