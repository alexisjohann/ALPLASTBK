Title: Sanctioning Systems for Norm Violation
Authors: Ernst Fehr
Journal: Evolution and Human Behavior

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper analyzes different sanctioning systems for norm violations.
We compare formal and informal sanctions, peer punishment and third-party
punishment, and examine when each is most effective. Understanding sanctioning
systems is key for institutional design and norm maintenance.
