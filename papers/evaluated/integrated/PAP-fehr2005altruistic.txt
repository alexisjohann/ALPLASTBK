Authors: Ernst Fehr, Urs Fischbacher
Journal: Advances in Economic Analysis & Policy
Year: 2005

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper provides an economic analysis of altruistic punishment and its
implications beyond laboratory experiments. We discuss the evolutionary
origins, the economic consequences, and the policy implications of
altruistic punishment. Understanding this phenomenon is crucial for
institutional design and public policy.
