Title: Informal Sanctions and Norm Violations
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 1998

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how informal sanctions affect norm violations. We show
that informal punishment by peers can effectively deter norm violations even
without formal enforcement. The threat of social disapproval and ostracism
provides a powerful enforcement mechanism.
