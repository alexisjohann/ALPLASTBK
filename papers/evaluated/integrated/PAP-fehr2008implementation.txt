ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This handbook chapter examines how behavioral economic insights can be
effectively implemented in policy. We discuss the challenges and best
practices for translating behavioral research into practical interventions.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. IMPLEMENTATION CHALLENGES
   - Lab-to-field translation
   - Scaling effects
   - Context sensitivity

2. BEST PRACTICES
   - Piloting and testing
   - Stakeholder engagement
   - Adaptive implementation

3. SUCCESS FACTORS
   - Institutional buy-in
   - Clear measurement
   - Iterative refinement

KEY INSIGHT:

context, piloting, and adaptive management; lab findings don't automatically
translate to policy success.
