Title: Designing for Behavioral Change: Lessons from Economics
Authors: Ernst Fehr
Journal: Journal of Economic Psychology
Year: 2012

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how to design effective behavioral change interventions.
Drawing lessons from economics, we discuss principles for creating
interventions that account for psychological and social factors.

1. DESIGN PRINCIPLES

2. INTERVENTION TYPES
   - Nudges and defaults
