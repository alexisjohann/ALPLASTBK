Title: Leadership and Cooperation in Public Goods Games
Authors: Ernst Fehr
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2006

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how leadership affects cooperation in public goods games.
We show that leaders can significantly increase cooperation by leading by
example and by communicating expectations. Leadership is particularly
effective when leaders are perceived as legitimate and trustworthy.
