Title: Market Experiences and Attitude Toward Markets
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Perspectives
Year: 2011

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This JEP article examines how market experiences shape attitudes toward
markets. We show that participation in markets affects social preferences
and norms, with implications for understanding market institutions and
their broader social effects.
