Title: Selection of Social Preferences in Large Groups
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2005

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how social preferences are selected in large groups.
We show that endogenous group formation allows cooperative types to sort
together, improving group outcomes. Selection mechanisms play a crucial
role in sustaining cooperation at scale.
