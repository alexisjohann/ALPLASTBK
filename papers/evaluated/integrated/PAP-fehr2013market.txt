Title: Gifts and Wages: Behavioural Reactions to Reciprocity
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Perspectives
Year: 2013

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how workers respond to wage "gifts" from employers.
We analyze the gift exchange hypothesis and evidence for reciprocal
responses to generous wages.
