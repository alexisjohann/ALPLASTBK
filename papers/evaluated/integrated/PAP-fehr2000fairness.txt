Title: Fairness and Retaliation
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Perspectives
Year: 2000

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines the relationship between fairness perceptions and
retaliatory behavior. We analyze how perceived unfairness triggers
punishment responses and how this shapes economic interactions.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. FAIRNESS VIOLATIONS
   - Trigger retaliatory responses
   - Proportional to perceived unfairness
   - Context-dependent thresholds

2. RETALIATION MECHANISMS
   - Costly punishment
   - Negative reciprocity
   - Emotional underpinnings

3. ECONOMIC IMPLICATIONS
   - Contract enforcement
   - Workplace behavior
   - Market institutions
