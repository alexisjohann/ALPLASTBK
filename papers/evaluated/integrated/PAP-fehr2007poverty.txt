================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how poverty affects fairness preferences. We review
evidence on whether the poor have different social preferences than the
wealthy, and how economic circumstances shape cooperative behavior.
Understanding these relationships is crucial for poverty alleviation.
