ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This handbook chapter examines patterns of punishment in community enforcement.
We review experimental evidence on how communities punish norm violators and
maintain cooperation. Understanding these patterns is crucial for designing
effective enforcement mechanisms.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. PUNISHMENT PATTERNS
   - Proportional to violation
   - Decentralized execution
   - Emotional component

2. COMMUNITY DYNAMICS
   - Reputation effects
   - Observation matters
   - Coordination on norms

3. EFFECTIVENESS
   - Deters violation
   - Sustains cooperation
   - Builds trust
