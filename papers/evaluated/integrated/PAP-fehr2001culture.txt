Title: Cultural Evolution of Cooperative Norms
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Theoretical Biology
Year: 2001

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how cooperative norms evolve through cultural transmission.
We develop a model where norms spread through social learning and are
maintained by punishment of violators. Cultural group selection can favor
groups with strong cooperative norms, even when such norms are costly to
individuals.
