ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This handbook chapter reviews theories of fairness and reciprocity and their
empirical support. We discuss the Fehr-Schmidt model, Bolton-Ockenfels ERC,
and intention-based reciprocity models. We examine new evidence and suggest
future research directions for modeling social preferences.
