Title: The Long-Run Problem of the Tragedy of the Commons
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter, Georg Kirchsteiger
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 1997

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines the long-run dynamics of common pool resource problems.
We show that while initial cooperation may occur, it tends to decline over
time without appropriate institutional arrangements. Understanding these
dynamics is crucial for designing sustainable resource management systems.
