Title: Cooperation at the Scale of Large Groups
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Nature
Year: 2008

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This Nature article examines how cooperation scales to large groups. We
discuss the mechanisms that enable cooperation among strangers in large
societies, including punishment, reputation, and institutions. Human
cooperation at scale is a remarkable evolutionary achievement.
