Title: Cultural Variations in Altruistic Punishment
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Science
Year: 2015

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This Science article examines cultural variations in altruistic punishment.
We analyze how punishment behavior differs across societies and what explains
these differences in terms of cultural and institutional factors.

================================================================================

1. CULTURAL VARIATION
   - Punishment levels vary by society
   - Market integration correlates
   - Institutional differences matter

2. EXPLANATORY FACTORS
   - Trust levels

3. IMPLICATIONS
   - Universal mechanisms, local variation
   - Cultural evolution
   - Policy adaptation needed
