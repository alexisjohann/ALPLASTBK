================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines the relationship between unemployment and wage fairness.
We argue that fairness concerns help explain wage rigidity and persistent
unemployment. When workers perceive wages as unfair, they reduce effort,
creating incentives for firms to pay above market-clearing wages.
