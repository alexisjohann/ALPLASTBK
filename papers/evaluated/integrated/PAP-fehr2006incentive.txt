Title: When Social Norms Overpower Competition: Camp Participation Among East German Children after Reunification
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of the European Economic Association
Year: 2006

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how social norms can overpower competitive market forces.
Using the natural experiment of German reunification, we study how inherited
social norms affected behavior even after the institutional context changed.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. NORM PERSISTENCE
   - Social norms persist after institutional change
   - East German norms survived reunification
   - Slow adaptation to new context

2. COMPETITION VS. NORMS
   - Norms can dominate market incentives
   - Cultural inertia substantial
   - Generational transmission

3. NATURAL EXPERIMENT INSIGHTS
   - Reunification as shock
   - Before-after comparisons
   - Selection vs. socialization
