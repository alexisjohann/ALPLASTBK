Title: Fairness and Contract Design
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Armin Klein, Klaus M. Schmidt
Journal: Econometrica
Year: 2009

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This Econometrica paper examines how fairness concerns affect optimal contract
design. We develop a theory where contracts serve as reference points and
test it experimentally. The results show that contract design must account
for fairness perceptions and reference-dependent preferences.
