Title: Competition and Cooperation in Firms
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2009

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines the interplay between competition and cooperation within
firms. We show that internal competition can undermine cooperation while
external competition can enhance it. Understanding this dynamic is crucial
for organizational design and incentive systems.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. INTERNAL COMPETITION
   - Can undermine cooperation
   - Tournament effects
   - Sabotage risks

2. EXTERNAL COMPETITION
   - Can enhance in-group cooperation
   - Us vs them effects
   - Performance pressure

3. DESIGN IMPLICATIONS
   - Balance competition types
   - Team vs individual incentives
   - Culture matters
