Title: Fairness and Rent-Seeking: The Impact of Inequality on Economic Behavior
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter, Georg Kirchsteiger
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2000

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how inequality and fairness perceptions affect economic
behavior and rent-seeking. We show that perceived unfairness of inequality
can lead to destructive rent-seeking behavior, reducing efficiency.
Understanding these dynamics is crucial for institutional design.
