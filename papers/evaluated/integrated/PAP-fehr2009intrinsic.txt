Title: Cooperation and the Removal of Monitoring Systems - Long-Run Lessons
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Bettina Rockenbach
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2009

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines what happens when monitoring systems are removed. We show
that removing monitoring can actually increase cooperation if it signals trust.
The long-run effects depend on how the removal is perceived - as trust or
as reduced enforcement capacity.
