Title: Cross-Cultural Variation in Cooperation Norms
Authors: Ernst Fehr
Journal: Journal of Cross-Cultural Psychology
Year: 2003

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines cross-cultural variation in cooperation norms using
experimental methods. We show that while cooperation exists universally,
its patterns and levels vary significantly across cultures. Understanding
this variation is crucial for cross-cultural management and policy.
