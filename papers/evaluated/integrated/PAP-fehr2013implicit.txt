Title: Implicit Fairness Preferences

Journal: Judgment and Decision Making
Year: 2013

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines implicit fairness preferences and how they operate
below conscious awareness. We analyze methods for measuring implicit
fairness and their relationship to explicit preferences.

1. IMPLICIT PREFERENCES

2. MEASUREMENT METHODS
