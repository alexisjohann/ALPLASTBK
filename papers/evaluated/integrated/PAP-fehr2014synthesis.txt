ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This Annual Review synthesis integrates findings from behavioral economics
research. We provide a unified framework for understanding key behavioral
phenomena and their economic implications.

   - Context effects pervasive
   - Heterogeneity fundamental

2. KEY PHENOMENA
   - Fairness and reciprocity
   - Present bias
   - Reference dependence

3. ECONOMIC IMPLICATIONS
   - Market outcomes affected

   - Institutional design
