================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper applies insights from cooperation research to global environmental
problems. We examine how strong reciprocity, social norms, and institutional
design can help address the tragedy of the commons in global pollution
contexts. Understanding human cooperation motives is essential for designing
effective environmental policies.
