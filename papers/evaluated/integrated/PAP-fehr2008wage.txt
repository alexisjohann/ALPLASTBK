Title: Behaviorally-Dependent Labor Supply: You Get What You Pay For
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter, Georg Kirchsteiger
Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2008

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how labor supply depends on behavioral factors, particularly
reciprocity. We show that "you get what you pay for" - fair wages elicit
higher effort through reciprocal motivation.
