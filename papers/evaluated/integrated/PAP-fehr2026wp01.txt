ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This working paper presents the Context-Dependent Complementarity framework
as a metatheoretical approach for integrating behavioral economics. Based on
Chapter 1 of the Evidence-Based Framework (EBF) documentation, we formalize
how context (Ψ) and complementarity (γ) interact to shape economic behavior.
