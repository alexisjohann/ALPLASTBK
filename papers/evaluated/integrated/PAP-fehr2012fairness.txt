Title: Fairness and the Principle of Pareto Efficiency

Journal: Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization
Year: 2012

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines the relationship between fairness and Pareto efficiency.
We analyze when fairness concerns conflict with efficiency and when they
can be reconciled.
