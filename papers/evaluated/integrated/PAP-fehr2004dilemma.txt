ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This handbook chapter reviews experimental research on social dilemmas and
cooperation. We examine prisoner's dilemmas, public goods games, common pool
resources, and other social dilemmas. Key findings on conditional cooperation,
punishment, and institutional design are synthesized.
