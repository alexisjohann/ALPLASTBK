Title: The Impact of Organizational Structure and Process on Outcomes
Authors: Ernst Fehr
Journal: Strategic Management Journal
Year: 2010

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines how organizational structure and processes affect
outcomes. We analyze how incentive design, hierarchy, and coordination
mechanisms interact with behavioral factors to shape organizational
performance.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. STRUCTURAL EFFECTS
   - Hierarchy affects motivation
   - Process design matters
   - Coordination mechanisms vary

2. INCENTIVE INTERACTIONS
   - Bonuses and intrinsic motivation
   - Team vs. individual incentives
   - Fairness in compensation

3. PERFORMANCE IMPLICATIONS
   - Structure-performance link
   - Behavioral moderators
   - Implementation challenges
