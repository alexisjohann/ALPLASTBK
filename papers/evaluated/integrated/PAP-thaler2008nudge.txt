ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paradigm-shifting book introduces LIBERTARIAN PATERNALISM and CHOICE
ARCHITECTURE as practical frameworks for improving decision-making while
preserving freedom of choice.

CORE CONCEPTS:

1. NUDGE: "Any aspect of the choice architecture that alters people's
   behavior in a predictable way without forbidding any options or
   significantly changing their economic incentives."

2. LIBERTARIAN PATERNALISM: It is both possible and legitimate for private
   and public institutions to steer people's choices while respecting
   freedom of choice.

3. CHOICE ARCHITECTURE: The design of different ways in which choices can
   be presented to decision makers. Elements include:
   - Defaults (most powerful tool)
   - Feedback
   - Mapping (translating choices to outcomes)
   - Structure of complex choices
   - Error expectation

KEY APPLICATIONS:
- SAVE MORE TOMORROW: Automatic enrollment + escalation in retirement savings
- Organ donation: Opt-out vs. opt-in systems
- Cafeteria design: Food placement affects healthy eating
- Energy conservation: Social comparison feedback
- Medicare Part D: Simplifying prescription drug choices

THE PHILOSOPHY: "A choice architect has the responsibility for organizing
the context in which people make decisions."
