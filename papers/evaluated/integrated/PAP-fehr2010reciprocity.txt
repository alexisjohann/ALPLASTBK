Title: Reciprocity and Market Integration - Did Smithian Thinking Fail?
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Journal of Economic Perspectives
Year: 2010

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines whether market integration undermines reciprocal behavior.
Contrary to concerns that markets erode social preferences, we show that
market participation and reciprocity can coexist.
