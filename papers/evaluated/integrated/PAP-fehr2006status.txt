Title: Status Quo Bias and Inequality Aversion
Authors: Ernst Fehr, Simon Gächter
Journal: Experimental Economics
Year: 2006

================================================================================
ABSTRACT
================================================================================

This paper examines the interaction between status quo bias and inequality
aversion. We show that people's aversion to inequality interacts with their
preference for the status quo, creating complex behavioral patterns. This
has implications for policy design and institutional change.
