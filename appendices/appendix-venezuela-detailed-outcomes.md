## The Fundamental Insight

> **Probability-weighted Expected Outcome:**
> 
> The intervention worsens K (coherence) in Venezuela, the region, and globally. 
> There is no scenario in which all actors are better off than before the intervention.
> 
> **This is not a zero-sum game – it is a negative-sum game.**

$$E[\Delta K_{total}] = \sum_{i} P_i \cdot \Delta K_i < 0 \quad \forall \text{ scenarios}$$

## Conclusion

The Ψ-Framework analysis demonstrates that:

1. **No positive-sum outcome exists** across all actor groups
2. **Coherence destruction is rapid**; reconstruction is slow and uncertain
3. **Feedback loops** create self-reinforcing instability
4. **Second-order effects** extend far beyond Venezuela
5. **The fundamental constraint** is endogenous legitimacy, which cannot be imported

The expected outcome is a net reduction in systemic coherence at all levels: local, regional, and global.
